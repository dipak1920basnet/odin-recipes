Burger

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef (80/20 lean)
  • 4 burger buns
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Red onion slices
  • Ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Form ground beef into 4 equal patties and season both sides with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook patties for about 3â€“4 minutes per side or until desired doneness.
  4. Place a slice of cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  5. Toast buns lightly with butter on the grill or pan.
  6. Assemble burgers with lettuce, tomato, onion, and your favorite condiments. Serve immediately.
