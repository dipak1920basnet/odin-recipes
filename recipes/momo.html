MOMO

Items:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (for dough)
  • Water (as needed for kneading)
  • 1/2 pound ground chicken or vegetables (cabbage, carrots, etc.)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, mix flour with water to form a smooth dough. Cover and let rest for 30 minutes.
  2. In another bowl, combine ground meat or vegetables, onion, garlic, ginger, salt, and pepper.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each into a thin circle (about 3 inches in diameter).
  4. Place 1 spoonful of filling in the center of each wrapper.
  5. Fold and pinch the edges to seal the momo, forming a pouch or half-moon shape.
  6. Prepare a steamer and lightly oil the tray to prevent sticking.
  7. Arrange momos in the steamer without overcrowding.
  8. Steam for 10â€“12 minutes until the wrappers become translucent and cooked through.
  9. Serve hot with spicy tomato chutney or dipping sauce.
