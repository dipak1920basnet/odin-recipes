Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet and cook the ground beef until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add marinara sauce to the beef and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, and meat sauce. Repeat layers and top with Parmesan.
  7. Bake for 25–30 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden. Let rest before serving.
