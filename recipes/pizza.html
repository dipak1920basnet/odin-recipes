Pizza

Items:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni (optional)
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the surface of the dough.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  6. Add pepperoni, bell peppers, olives, or any other toppings you like.
  7. Bake the pizza for 10–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven and let cool for a couple of minutes.
  9. Slice and serve hot!
